      Press H to toggle instruction
      1. Controls
        • W A S D — move or attack in that direction.
        • E then W/A/S/D — interact with a nearby tile in that direction.
        • Space — wait one turn.
        • I — open inventory.
        • J — open quest log.
        • Hotkeys: 1–9 and 0 — use items assigned to these slots.
      2. Movement and Attack Rules
        1. If the tile in the chosen direction is passable and there is no NPC or wall, the player moves into that tile.
        2. If there is an NPC in that direction, pressing the direction key attacks that NPC instead of moving.
        3. Walls and non-crossable tiles block movement.
      3. Interaction
        1. Press E then a direction key (W/A/S/D) to interact with the tile in that direction.
      4. Item Types and How to Use Them
        • Projectable — select its hotkey, then click a target tile to fire a projectile there.
        • Touchable — select its hotkey then press W/A/S/D to use on an adjacent tile in that direction, or press Space to use on the player's own tile.
        • Equipment — select its hotkey to equip it; the game automatically places it in the correct equipment slot.
      5. Inventory and Hotkey Assignment
        1. Open inventory with I.
        2. Navigate inventory items with A (left) and D (right).
        3. To assign the currently selected inventory item to a hotkey, press the hotkey number 1–9 or 0.
        4. New items added to inventory are placed into the next available hotkey slot automatically when possible.
        5. Once an item is assigned to a hotkey, use it by pressing that hotkey (follow rules for projectable/touchable/equipment).
      6. Quest Log
        1. Press J to open the quest log and see tasks and progress.
      7. Quick Summary
        • W/A/S/D — move or attack.
        • E + direction — interact.
        • Space — wait / use on self (for touchable).
        • I — inventory; A/D to pick item, then press 1–0 to assign.
        • Select a hotkey to use: click a tile for projectable, directional key/space for touchable, hotkey to equip equipment.
